About Us

Founded in 2020 in the heart of the Philippines, The Sovereign Dial was created with a singular mission: to introduce and elevate the world of luxury watches to the Philippine market. Inspired by prestigious watch retailers such as Lucerne, we aimed to build a platform where discerning customers could explore, discover, and acquire the finest timepieces from around the globe.

At The Sovereign Dial, we pride ourselves on offering a carefully selected collection of high-end watches from some of the most celebrated and respected watchmakers in the industry. Our range includes timepieces known for their unparalleled craftsmanship, precision, and timeless design. Whether you're a seasoned horology enthusiast or new to the world of luxury watches, our team is dedicated to providing expert guidance and personalized service to help you find the perfect watch that suits your style, personality, and needs.

We understand that a luxury watch is more than just a timekeeper — it is a statement of style, sophistication, and individuality. That is why we ensure that every timepiece in our collection represents the epitome of quality and artistry.

At The Sovereign Dial, we are not just about selling watches — we are about sharing the passion for horology, educating our clients, and building a community that appreciates the beauty and precision of fine watchmaking. With a commitment to excellence, integrity, and unparalleled customer service, The Sovereign Dial has quickly become a trusted name for luxury watches in the Philippines.

Whether you're making your first luxury watch purchase or adding to a growing collection, we invite you to explore The Sovereign Dial — where time is not just told, but celebrated.
